Scrambled Eggs

eggs are one of my favorite food.Here is a ricepe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

1.melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
2.Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
3.once butter has multed add cream and eggs
4.using a spatula fold the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds(as if you are making an omelette)
5.when the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
